Who made this?

My name is Anthony. I like coding in Python. I don't have a background in coding or development so have had to learn everything myself. As you might imagine, it involved a lot of trips to Google and Stack Overflow.

Why?

I broke my arm and had a lot of time on my hands. Well, hand. I needed a project, and was interested to see how Django, PostgreSQL and Heroku work. By the way, yes I really have typed this all with one hand.

How does it work?

The bot collects headlines from selected tech-related subreddits and uses these to build markov chains. These are fed to the homepage here for voting on. Every so often the bot pulls the most popular sentence from the pool and tweets it.

Why are some sentences so bad?

They are 100% automatically generated. The bot knows how to form a sentence (sort of) but doesn't understand the idea of making sense. Also until it's collected enough input sentences things may be a little janky.

Can I see the code?

Yes, it's on github. There's also a link in the footer of each page. Or, if you really can't be arsed to scroll down, you could try clicking here, although I wouldn't necessarily advise it.
